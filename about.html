About me

Designer by craft, developer by instinct

I'm Salman, a UI/UX designer with 4+ years of industry experience and a Computer Science background. I currently design at Chillybin Web Design, an award-winning agency with offices in Singapore and Australia, where I've built design systems and shipped end-to-end digital products across healthcare, real estate, finance and philanthropy.

What sets me apart is the handoff that never happens: I design in Figma and then build the real thing in code. That means tighter feedback loops, fewer compromises, and interfaces that look in production exactly like they did in the mockup. Research-led and outcome-focused, I care most about the number that moves after launch.
